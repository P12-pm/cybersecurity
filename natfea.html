IT Hygiene

IT Hygiene refers to the best practices and measures that help to maintain the security, availability, and efficiency of an organization's IT infrastructure. Threat actors typically take advantage of security weaknesses when there is poor IT hygiene. Organizations need to implement good IT hygiene practices to prevent cyberattacks, data breaches, and other security threats.
